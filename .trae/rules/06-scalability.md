\# Scalability Rules



Build for multi-firm SaaS growth without breaking current stability.



Rules:

\- design features to support multiple firms cleanly

\- avoid tight coupling between modules

\- prefer reusable services over duplicated logic

\- keep queries efficient and indexed

\- avoid loading large datasets unnecessarily

\- support feature flags and permission-based rollout

\- preserve auditability during scaling

\- avoid architecture that only works for one founder test firm



When designing new modules, think about:

\- tenant safety

\- performance

\- maintainability

\- future extension

\- backward compatibility
