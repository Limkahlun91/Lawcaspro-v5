\# LawcasPro Business Rules



Lawcaspro-v5 is focused on Malaysian legal operations, especially conveyancing, loan, SPA, finance, approvals, and document automation.



Current priority:

\- conveyancing and transaction workflows

\- finance hub and payment voucher controls

\- document automation with permission control

\- AI as assistant only, not final decision maker



Business rules:

\- litigation is not current priority unless requested

\- payment vouchers require statuses, timestamps, actor tracking, and traceability

\- generated documents must be versioned and traceable

\- AI suggestions must remain reviewable by authorized users

\- founder is platform creator, not default partner of every firm

\- founder access to firm data requires explicit consent or controlled support access

\- all support/emergency access must be logged



Additional Lawcaspro business guardrails:

\- founder is platform-level only, never implicitly treated as a firm partner

\- founder debug workspace access must be explicit, time-bound, consented, and logged

\- finance actions must preserve actor, timestamp, status history, and auditability

\- generated documents must preserve version traceability and actor linkage

\- no feature may assume only one test firm exists
